嗨，大家好！我是一名熱愛手作和運動的大學生。在我的空閒時間，我喜歡利用手作創造各種有趣的手工藝品，從編織到繪畫都是我熱愛的項目。此外，我也很喜歡運動，特別是跑步和跳舞。運動不僅讓我保持身體健康，還能讓我釋放壓力和放鬆身心。

Hello everyone! My name is Yuan Ya and I am a college student who loves crafting and sports. During my free time, I enjoy using my hands to create various interesting handicrafts, including knitting and painting. Crafting is one of my favorite hobbies as it allows me to express my creativity and make unique items. In addition to crafting, I am also passionate about sports, particularly running and dancing. Engaging in sports helps me to maintain my physical health and also helps me to unwind and relax my mind and body. It's truly amazing how much stress and tension can be released through physical activity.
